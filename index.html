Mileage is one of the easiest deductions. Every business drive = 70¢/mile off your taxable income. No tracking gas — just log the miles. The IRS requires: date, destination, business purpose, miles. This app is your official log.
Counts: Client meetings, networking events, scouting school locations, supply runs. Doesn't count: Regular commuting. Since you work from home, most business driving qualifies!
